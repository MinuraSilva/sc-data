• 5. Pairs on Truths (Sacca Yamaka)

5.1.1. Summary Chapter on Terms (Paṇṇattivāra Uddesa)

PTS 5.1(There are) four truths: Suffering-truth, Origination- Cessation-truth and Path-truth.
